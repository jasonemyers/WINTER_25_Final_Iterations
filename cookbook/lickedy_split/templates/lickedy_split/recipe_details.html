Tiramisu
A classic Italian no-bake dessert made with espresso-soaked ladyfingers layered with mascarpone cream and dusted with cocoa powder.
Chill for at least 6 hours before serving for best texture and flavor. Can be made a day in advance.
Dessert Italian Coffee No Bake
30
0
6 egg yolks
3/4 cup granulated sugar
2/3 cup milk
1 1/4 cups heavy cream
8 oz mascarpone cheese
1 cup strong brewed coffee (cooled)
1 package ladyfinger cookies
2 tbsp cocoa powder
  1. In a saucepan, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until smooth. Stir in milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until slightly thickened (about 10 minutes). Cool slightly and chill in fridge.
  2. Whip cream until stiff peaks form. In another bowl, beat mascarpone cheese until smooth.
  3. Fold chilled egg mixture into mascarpone, then fold in whipped cream.
  4. Dip ladyfingers in coffee quickly and layer in a 9x13 inch dish.
  5. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers. Repeat layers and top with sifted cocoa powder.
  6. Refrigerate at least 6 hours or overnight before serving.
